Les irrésistibles Focal

"Les Irrésistibles Focal" campaign was made by Conversationnel for Focal.

Focal-JMlab is a French company, specialized in designing and selling of high-fidelity audio systems.

To enhance the engagement of their fans on the Facebook page and the sales of their products on the e-commerce website, Focal started with Conversationnel a social media campaign called "Les Irrésistibles".

The campaign highlighted, 25 featured products selected by the brand via social networks and e-commerce website. Each product had a dedicated publication, and could be unlocked through the participation and commitment of fans on Facebook. Finally, the unlocked products could be won by some of the fans at the end of the campaign.

The graphic identity of the campaign "Les Irrésistibles" was developed by the Artistic Director of Conversationnel, then I've made the publications and videos.

After a 4-months campaign and a almost 30 Facebook publications, the campaign ended with 24 unlocked lots, over 13 300 interactions on Facebook and 8 065 new fans.

View the campaign on Conversationnel website.

  • Date: October 2015 - January 2016
  • Client: Focal
  • Category: Brand Content Facebook
  • Agency: Conversationnel

Datatopia 3.0

Creation of a virtual exhibition website for Datatopia 3.0 project.

Datatopia 3.0 developed by Château Fort Fort agency is an art research project based on error and randomness. The idea started by a random pictures found on the web, and brought his creator to randomly realize 3 forms of creation: a nagging melody, a 3D maze and a tangible object.

This creation steps set up a fourth form of creation: a virtual exhibition website about this project.

I fully built the exhibition website: technological and graphic watch, web design, video creation, dubbing, and website production.

See the website Datatopia 3.0.

Wamp, Sublime Text, Audacity, Illustrator, Premiere, Photoshop,...
